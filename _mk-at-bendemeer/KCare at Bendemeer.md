<h4>About Kcare at Bendemeer</h4>
<p>Our KCare operator is <strong>Big Heart Student Care</strong>, a school-based
service for children enrolled in MOE Kindergartens (MKs) in Singapore.
Operating from Monday to Friday, including school holidays, KCare provides
a seamless and convenient care environment beyond the four-hour MK session.</p>
<p>The programme supports childrenâ€™s holistic development through a range
of engaging modular activities, such <strong>as cookery, music and movement, dramatisation, tinkering, gardening, sand and water play, and arts and crafts.</strong>
</p>
