<p>The HI-Light Programme is an integrated framework that focuses on holistic
development across six key learning areas: <strong>Aesthetics and Creative Expression, Discovery of the World, Language and Literacy, Motor Skills, Numeracy, and Social and Emotional Development.</strong>
</p>

<p>The Hi-Light Programme nurtures values, skills, and positive learning
dispositions through play and exploration, providing young children with
rich and meaningful learning experiences.</p>
