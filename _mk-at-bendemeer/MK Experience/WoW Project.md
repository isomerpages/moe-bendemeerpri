<p>The Weeks of Wonder (WoW) Project involves <strong>collaborative, child-led investigations </strong>in
which children work alongside their peers and teachers to explore topics
of interest. Through these projects, children build <strong>confidence, problem-solving skills, and communication skills</strong> in
both English and their Mother Tongue Language (MTL).
<br>
</p>
