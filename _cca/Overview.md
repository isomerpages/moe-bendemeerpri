<h4>CCA Schedule</h4>
<p>During curriculum time: Every Tuesday, 7.30am - 9.00am
<br>After school: According to the details given in the respective consent
forms</p>
<p>The Overall CCA Programme in the school consists of Modular CCAs and Niche
CCAs.</p>
<p>Modular CCAs are introduced to all students. &nbsp;In alignment to PERI
recommendation of the Programme for Active Learning (PAL) for lower Primary,
PAL will be subsumed as part of the Modular CCAs for Primary 1 and Primary
2, with a total of 2 hours per week. &nbsp;For Primary 3 to Primary 6 students,
Modular CCAs aims to encourage mass participation in sports enrichment,
visual and performing arts enrichment, ICT programming and life skills
programmes, with a total of 1.5 hours per week.</p>
<p>There are 3 different types of Niche CCAs, namely Sports and Games, Performing
Arts and Clubs. Under Sports and Games, our school offers Floorball, Rope
Skipping, Sepak Takraw, Track and Field and Wushu. Under Performing Arts,
our school offers Concert Band, Chinese, Malay and Indian Dances. &nbsp;For
Niche Clubs, our school offers Art Club as well as Media and Environment
Club.</p>
<p>Niche CCAs are offered to selected students who are identified to participate
in these CCAs. These students will be trained for both internal and external
competitions and performances. &nbsp;Depending on the training schedules,
Niche CCAs may be conducted during Modular CCA timing and/or outside curriculum
hours. &nbsp;Selected students will receive a Niche CCA Option Form, along
with consent forms to inform parents of the training schedules and activities,
particularly when training is conducted outside curriculum hours.</p>

<p>Overall CCA Programme is a 6 year programme to:</p>
