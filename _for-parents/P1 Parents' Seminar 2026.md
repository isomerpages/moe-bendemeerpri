<h3>Primary 1 Parents’ Seminar 2026 – Briefing Slides</h3>
<p>Thank you to all parents for attending the Primary 1 Parents’ Seminar
2026 held on Friday, 2 January 2026. We appreciate your time, presence,
and continued support as we work in partnership to support your child’s
transition into Primary 1 at Bendemeer Primary School.</p>
<p>For your reference, the PDF versions of the presentation slides shared
during the seminar have been uploaded below.</p>
<p>The seminar covered key areas to support parents in understanding their
child’s learning journey, including:</p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Character and Citizenship Education (CCE) engagement,</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Overviews of the Primary 1 English Language, Mathematics, and Mother Tongue
Language programmes, and</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Guidance on EdTech parenting, with practical tips to support learning
at home.</p>
</li>
</ul>
