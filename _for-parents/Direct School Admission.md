<h3><strong>2026 DSA-Sec Exercise</strong></h3>
<p>The <strong>Direct School Admission (DSA-Sec) Exercise 2026</strong> will
commence on <strong>6 May 2026</strong>.</p>
<p><strong>Application Period</strong>
<br>6 May 2026 (11:00 am) to 2 June 2026 (4:30 pm)</p>
<p>Parents may apply through the DSA-Sec Portal using <strong>one parent’s Singpass</strong>.</p>
<p>If you require assistance with the application, please approach our <strong>General Office</strong> for
support.</p>
