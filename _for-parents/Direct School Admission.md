<h4><strong>2026 DSA-Sec Exercise</strong></h4>
<p>The Direct School Admission (DSA-Sec) Exercise 2026 will commence on 6
May 2026.</p>
<p><strong><u>Application Period</u></strong>
<br>6 May 2026 (11:00 am) to 2 June 2026 (4:30 pm)
<br>Parents may apply through the DSA-Sec Portal using one parent’s Singpass.
<br>If you require assistance with the application, please approach our General
Office for support.</p>
